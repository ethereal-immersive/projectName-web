About

In the span of nine months, our team, Ethereal, plans on creating an immersive digital art exhibit for our senior capstone project. With a user-centered design process, we hope to successfully achieve our goal of the project; we believe that an immersive digital media exhibit that is dynamically affected by the outcome of different biosensors for residents of the Philadelphia area will facilitate spontaneous creative collaboration between strangers. We’ll know this is true when we see participants communicating with each other to achieve an outcome.

Three people can enter the exhibit at a time. They will be given heart rate sensors to raise or lower their heart rate in order to ascend or descend to five different depths of the ocean: shallow seas, coral reef, open ocean, caves, and deep sea.

We are using TouchDesigner to build our exhibit. Additionally, we are using Arduino boards to build custom heart rate sensors specifically designed and programmed to control the art. Participants will wear the heart rate sensors in the form of bracelets during the exhibit.
